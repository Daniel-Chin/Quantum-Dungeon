BERT corridor
    一行字与走廊平行。  
    you walk forwards or backwards. 
    text is generated as your vision area moves. 
    if specific words appear, trasures appear with them. 
    You can manipulate by walking forwards and backwards. 
    Maybe even spawn splits, where two roads diverge.
